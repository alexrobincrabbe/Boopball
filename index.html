The   Game

  • To score, pass the ball through the hoop, from above
  • Score five hoops to proceed to the next level
  • Each level has a time limit of 60 seconds (can be changed in settings)
  • Win by completing all 5 levels before you run out of time!

How   to   Play

  • Click/tap Booper to begin
  • When the arrow points in the direction you want, quickly click/tap Booper
  • Holding the click/tap will increase throwing power
  • Letting go releases the ball
  • Click/tap to retrieve the dropped ball
  • To pause the game press Escape, or swipe right
