The   Game

  • To score, pass the ball through the hoop, from above
  • Score five hoops to proceed to the next level
  • Each level has a time limit of 60 seconds
  • Win by completing all 5 levels before you run out of time!

How   to   Play

  • Click/tap on Booper to throw the ball
  • Hold the click/tap to increase throwing power
  • Choose which direction to throw and release the ball
  • Tap to retrieve the dropped ball
