Instructions

  • The Goal is to get the ball to pass through the hoop from above.
  • There are 4 levels to complete.
  • Each Level has a time limit of 60 seconds.
  • You must score 5 hoops to complete a level.

Controls

  • To throw the ball tap (click) on the character.
  • Hold the tap(click) to in crease the throw power.
  • You can also tap to retrieve the ball after it is thrown.
